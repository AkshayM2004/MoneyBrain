What is MoneyBrain?

MoneyBrain is an online financial calculator built for engineering students, MBA students, and professionals who regularly solve numerical finance problems.

It supports EMI calculation, interest rate detection, NPV, IRR, EOQ, WACC, CAGR, break-even analysis, inventory management, and investment decisions.

  • Exam and interview preparation
  • Professionals evaluating loans and investments
  • Anyone learning financial management
