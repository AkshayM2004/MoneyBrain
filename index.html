What is MoneyBrain?

MoneyBrain is an online financial calculator designed for engineering students, MBA students, and professionals who regularly solve numerical finance problems. Instead of selecting formulas manually, users can paste a finance-related question and get instant answers.

It supports EMI calculation, interest rate detection, future value, NPV, IRR, EOQ, WACC, CAGR, break-even analysis, inventory management, and investment decisions.

Who should use this financial calculator?

  • Engineering and MBA students
  • Students preparing for exams and interviews
  • Professionals evaluating investments and loans
  • Anyone learning financial management concepts

Why MoneyBrain is different

Traditional calculators require users to know the correct formula in advance. MoneyBrain automatically detects the problem type from natural language input and applies the correct financial logic instantly.

This makes MoneyBrain especially useful for beginners who want to understand finance concepts without memorizing complex formulas.
